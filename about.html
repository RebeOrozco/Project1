ABOUT ME!

Hello, my name is Rebecca Orozco.
Growing up I was always drawn to the wonderful world of creativity. I have always loved to learn new crafts and art skills. My love for art started when I was younger having to spend my summer at summer camps where we would often do arts and craft. As time went on, I got introduced to social media which showed me so much more art and crafty activities from all over the world. I was in awe and inspired.
With my love of creating and picking up different art related hobbies I decided to pursue Architecture my first year at UNLV. Let's just say it was a learning experience and I'm glad I followed a different path. I realized Graphic Design was what I wanted to do and ever since, it has been a bit stressful at times, but it ha been so fun and worth it.

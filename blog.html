RECENT ARTWORK

Likeness Practice

2024
Digital Drawing

I enjoy studying the art of drawing faces. Here I practiced capturing the real likeness of a Kpop idol named Choi Beomgyu. In this drawing I focused on using lines to measure out the right places where the facial features belonged. I also played around with a pen like brush to create detailed little strokes to pull the piece together. I also experimented with the background colors and blurs to make my subject appear to be glowing.

Band Tour Poster

2025
Digital Design

Here I designed a Tour poster for the Kpop group, BTS' last tour in 2021-2022. I drew inspiration from the tour's theme of orange and fun shapes. In the poster I wanted to make it inviting for fans and include collage like stickers and confetti like texture in the background. To add more of the band's style, I incorporated their logo as a cut out where the members look like they're popping out from.

Zine Spread

2024
Digital Design

We were tasked to create a zine spread from a made up magazine company. We also needed to have a subject to revolve our spread around and I chose to focus on male fashion. I then centered my made up magazine around my subject with a cool blue and orange color palette. I had fun using different fonts and altering my subjects apprearnce either reflecting himself or fading away with the help of Photoshop.
